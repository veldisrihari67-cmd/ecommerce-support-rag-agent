Product Conditions and Return Exceptions Policy
1. Overview

This document defines the conditions under which products are eligible or ineligible for return, replacement, or refund. It also outlines exceptions based on product type, usage, hygiene concerns, and perishability.

The objective is to ensure fairness while maintaining product integrity and safety standards.

2. General Return Condition Requirements

To qualify for a return, products must:

Be unused and in original condition
Include original packaging, tags, and accessories
Not show signs of wear, damage, or alteration

Failure to meet these conditions may result in rejection of return requests.

3. Opened vs Unopened Products

Return eligibility depends on whether the product has been opened:

Unopened products: Generally eligible for return within the return window
Opened products: May be ineligible unless defective or damaged

Opened products are subject to stricter evaluation due to potential usage.

4. Used Products

Products that show signs of usage are typically not eligible for return.

Examples of usage include:

Physical wear and tear
Missing parts
Altered packaging
Installation or activation

Exceptions may apply if the product is defective.

5. Hygiene-Sensitive Products

Certain items cannot be returned once opened due to hygiene reasons.

Examples include:

Personal care products
Innerwear
Beauty and grooming items
Health-related products

If such items are opened or used:

Return requests are usually denied

Exceptions:

Damaged, defective, or incorrect item delivered
6. Perishable Products

Perishable goods include:

Food items
Beverages
Fresh or temperature-sensitive products

Return conditions:

Must be reported within 24 hours of delivery
Evidence of spoilage or damage is required

Returns may not be accepted due to logistics constraints, but refunds or replacements may be provided.

7. Damaged or Defective Products

If a product is received in damaged or defective condition:

Must be reported within the defined timeframe (24–48 hours)
Supporting evidence (images/videos) required

Eligible resolutions:

Replacement
Refund
8. Incorrect Product Delivered

If the customer receives a wrong item:

Must report within the return window
Provide proof of mismatch

Resolution:

Replacement or refund
9. Missing Components or Accessories

If parts are missing:

Must be reported within 48 hours
Evidence may be required

Resolution:

Replacement of missing components
Full replacement or refund if necessary
10. Non-Returnable Product Categories

Certain categories may be marked as non-returnable:

Customized or personalized products
Digital products or downloads
Gift cards
Clearance or final sale items

These items are not eligible for return unless defective.

11. Electronics and High-Value Items

For electronics:

Must be returned with original packaging and accessories
Serial numbers may be verified

If activated or used:

Return may be denied unless defective
12. Seal and Packaging Integrity

Products with tamper-proof seals:

Must not be opened for return eligibility
Broken seals may result in rejection

Exceptions:

Defective or damaged items
13. “Keep Item” Requests

Requests where customers ask to:

Keep the item
Receive a full refund

Such requests are generally denied unless:

Product is low-value
Return logistics are not feasible
Approved as a goodwill exception
14. Partial Damage Cases

If only part of the product is damaged:

Partial refund may be offered
Replacement of damaged part may be considered

Full refund may not be granted in such cases.

15. Product Condition Verification

Returned products are inspected for:

Physical condition
Completeness
Signs of usage

Outcomes:

Approved return
Partial refund
Rejection
16. Rejection of Returns

Returns may be rejected if:

Product is used or damaged by customer
Missing original packaging
Request made after return window
Evidence is insufficient

Rejected items may be sent back to the customer.

17. Exchange Conditions

Products eligible for exchange must:

Meet original return conditions
Be available in replacement inventory

Exchange may not be possible if stock is unavailable.

18. Seller-Specific Conditions

Marketplace sellers may define additional conditions.

However:

Platform policies take precedence in disputes
19. Fraud Prevention

The platform monitors:

Frequent return requests
Claims inconsistent with evidence

Suspicious activity may result in:

Return denial
Account restrictions
20. Exceptional Approvals

In rare cases, exceptions may be granted:

Based on customer history
Product category
Operational feasibility

Such approvals must be documented.

21. Customer Responsibility

Customers must:

Inspect products upon delivery
Maintain product condition for returns
Provide accurate information
22. Policy Updates

Product condition rules may be updated periodically.

23. Compliance Enforcement

All return requests are evaluated against this policy.

Non-compliance may result in denial of request.

24. Support Assistance

Customers may contact support for clarification on product eligibility.

25. Final Authority

Final decisions regarding product condition and return eligibility rest with the platform, based on policy guidelines and verification results.

Appendix A: Real-World Scenarios
Scenario 1: Used Product Sold as New
Situation: Customer receives a used item labeled as new
Policy Applied: Product condition accuracy
Decision: Full refund or replacement issued
Scenario 2: Damaged Product Sold Without Disclosure
Situation: Item has defects not mentioned
Policy Applied: Transparency policy
Decision: Return accepted; seller penalized
Scenario 3: Refurbished Product Misrepresented
Situation: Refurbished item sold as original
Policy Applied: Product classification
Decision: Listing corrected; refund provided
Scenario 4: Missing Accessories
Situation: Product delivered without included items
Policy Applied: Completeness requirement
Decision: Missing items shipped or partial refund
Scenario 5: Expired Product Delivered
Situation: Perishable item past expiry date
Policy Applied: Safety standards
Decision: Immediate refund and removal of listing
Scenario 6: Open-Box Item Not Declared
Situation: Product already opened before delivery
Policy Applied: Disclosure requirement
Decision: Return approved
Scenario 7: Product Quality Below Expectation
Situation: Item does not meet described quality
Policy Applied: Quality assurance
Decision: Return or exchange offered
Scenario 8: Counterfeit Product Delivered
Situation: Fake version of branded item
Policy Applied: Authenticity policy
Decision: Refund and seller suspension
Scenario 9: Product Condition Changes During Delivery
Situation: Item damaged in transit
Policy Applied: Delivery responsibility
Decision: Replacement provided
Scenario 10: Incorrect Condition Tag
Situation: Product marked as “new” instead of “used”
Policy Applied: Listing accuracy
Decision: Listing updated; compensation issued

Appendix C: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Q1: What does product condition mean?

A: It describes the state of the product (new, used, etc.).

Q2: Can I return a used product?

A: Only if it does not match description.

Q3: What is a refurbished product?

A: Repaired and tested item sold again.

Q4: Are all products new?

A: No, depends on listing.

Q5: What if product is damaged?

A: You can request return or replacement.

Q6: Are accessories included?

A: Yes, as mentioned in description.

Q7: What if product is fake?

A: Full refund and action against seller.

Q8: Can condition be wrong?

A: Rare, but can be reported.

Q9: What is open-box product?

A: Item that has been opened but unused.

Q10: Are expired products allowed?

A: No.

Q11: Can I verify product condition before buying?

A: Through description and images.

Q12: What if quality is poor?

A: Return or exchange is available.

Q13: Are refurbished items reliable?

A: Yes, if certified.

Q14: Can sellers mislabel products?

A: Not allowed; strict action taken.

Q15: What if product condition changes during delivery?

A: Replacement or refund provided.
