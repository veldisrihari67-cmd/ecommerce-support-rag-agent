Delivery Issues and Exception Handling Policy
1. Overview

This document defines how delivery-related issues and exceptions are handled on the platform. It addresses delays, failed deliveries, damaged goods in transit, and customer claims regarding delivery problems.

The goal is to ensure fair resolutions while maintaining operational feasibility and policy compliance.

2. Delivery Status Definitions

Orders may have the following delivery statuses:

Placed
Shipped
Out for Delivery
Delivered
Delivery Failed
Returned to Origin

Each status determines the actions available to customers and support teams.

3. Late Delivery

An order is considered delayed if it is not delivered by the estimated delivery date.

In such cases:

Customers may contact support for updates
Logistics partners are contacted for clarification

Possible resolutions:

Apology and status update
Shipping fee refund (in select cases)
Option to cancel (if not yet delivered)

Late delivery alone does not automatically qualify for a full refund.

4. Delivery of Perishable Items

Perishable items (e.g., food products) are subject to strict conditions:

Must be delivered within specified timeframes
Quality may degrade if delayed

If perishable items arrive damaged (e.g., melted, spoiled):

Customer must report within 24 hours
Evidence may be required

Resolution:

Replacement or refund based on verification

Requests to keep the item and receive a full refund may be denied depending on policy.

5. Damaged During Transit

If items are damaged during delivery:

Customer must report within 48 hours
Provide supporting images or videos

Resolution options:

Replacement
Refund

Repeated claims may trigger additional verification.

6. Delivery Marked as Completed but Not Received

If tracking shows “delivered” but customer has not received the item:

Customer must verify delivery address and surroundings
Platform checks courier proof (photo, signature, GPS data)

Possible outcomes:

Replacement
Refund
Claim denial if delivery is confirmed
7. Failed Delivery Attempts

Delivery may fail due to:

Customer unavailability
Incorrect address
Restricted access

After multiple attempts:

Order may be returned to origin
Customer may request re-delivery or refund
8. Refused Delivery

If a customer refuses delivery:

Order is marked as undelivered
Item is returned to warehouse

Refund processing begins after item is received back.

Shipping charges may be deducted depending on the reason for refusal.

9. Returned to Origin (RTO)

Orders may be returned to origin due to:

Failed delivery attempts
Address issues
Customer refusal

Once returned:

Refund is initiated
Replacement may be offered if requested
10. Partial Deliveries

In some cases, orders may be delivered in parts:

Customers are notified of split shipments
Missing items must be reported within 48 hours

Resolution:

Replacement or refund for missing items
11. Address Issues

Customers are responsible for providing accurate delivery details.

Incorrect or incomplete addresses may result in:

Delivery delays
Failed delivery
Additional charges
12. High-Value Item Deliveries

For high-value items:

OTP-based delivery confirmation may be required
Signature or ID verification may be needed

Failure to provide verification may delay delivery.

13. Delivery Exceptions Due to External Factors

Delays or failures may occur due to:

Weather conditions
Natural disasters
Government restrictions
Logistics disruptions

In such cases:

Delivery timelines may be extended
Refunds are not automatically guaranteed
14. Compensation and Goodwill

In certain cases, the platform may offer:

Partial refunds
Promotional credits
Future discount coupons

These are provided at the discretion of the platform.

15. Abuse Prevention

The platform monitors patterns such as:

Repeated claims of non-delivery
Frequent damage complaints

Actions may include:

Additional verification
Claim rejection
Account restriction
16. Escalation Handling

If delivery issues are not resolved:

Customers may request escalation
Case reviewed by senior support

Final decisions are based on evidence and policy guidelines.

17. Marketplace Orders

For seller-fulfilled orders:

Seller may be responsible for delivery issues
Platform may intervene if necessary

Resolution timelines may vary.

18. Policy Exceptions

Certain requests may fall outside policy, such as:

Requesting refund without returning item
Claims reported after allowed timeframe

Such requests may be denied or escalated for review.

19. Policy Updates

The platform reserves the right to update delivery-related policies.

20. Customer Support

Customers may contact support for delivery-related concerns. Resolution timelines typically range between 24–72 hours.


Appendix A: Real-World Scenarios
Scenario 1: Order Marked Delivered but Not Received
Situation: System shows delivered but customer did not receive package
Policy Applied: Delivery verification
Decision: Investigation initiated; refund or replacement issued if confirmed
Scenario 2: Late Delivery
Situation: Order delivered after expected date
Policy Applied: Delivery SLA
Decision: Compensation or apology provided
Scenario 3: Package Lost in Transit
Situation: Shipment cannot be tracked or located
Policy Applied: Logistics responsibility
Decision: Full refund or replacement
Scenario 4: Damaged Package on Arrival
Situation: Product damaged during shipping
Policy Applied: Transit protection
Decision: Free return and replacement
Scenario 5: Wrong Delivery Address
Situation: Customer enters incorrect address
Policy Applied: Address responsibility
Decision: Order may fail or be returned
Scenario 6: Delivery Attempt Failed
Situation: Delivery agent unable to deliver
Policy Applied: Retry mechanism
Decision: Reattempt scheduled
Scenario 7: Package Delivered to Neighbor
Situation: Delivered to nearby location
Policy Applied: Alternate delivery handling
Decision: Customer informed with proof
Scenario 8: Delivery Agent Misconduct
Situation: Unprofessional behavior reported
Policy Applied: Service quality policy
Decision: Action taken against agent
Scenario 9: Partial Delivery
Situation: Only some items delivered
Policy Applied: Order completeness
Decision: Missing items re-shipped
Scenario 10: Delivery Delay Due to Weather
Situation: Natural conditions affect delivery
Policy Applied: Force majeure
Decision: Delivery rescheduled

Appendix C: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Q1: What if my order is not delivered?

A: You can report it for investigation.

Q2: Can I track my delivery?

A: Yes, through tracking system.

Q3: What if delivery is delayed?

A: Wait or contact support.

Q4: What if package is damaged?

A: Request replacement or refund.

Q5: Can delivery be rescheduled?

A: Yes, depending on availability.

Q6: What if I am not available?

A: Delivery will be reattempted.

Q7: Can I change delivery address?

A: Only before shipment.

Q8: What if item is missing?

A: Report for reshipment.

Q9: Are delivery charges refundable?

A: Depends on issue type.

Q10: What is proof of delivery?

A: Signature or OTP confirmation.

Q11: What if wrong item delivered?

A: Return and replacement available.

Q12: Can someone else receive my order?

A: Yes, with authorization.

Q13: What if delivery agent misbehaves?

A: Report to customer support.

Q14: Why is delivery taking long?

A: Due to logistics or external factors.

Q15: Are international deliveries trackable?

A: Yes, but updates may be slower.
