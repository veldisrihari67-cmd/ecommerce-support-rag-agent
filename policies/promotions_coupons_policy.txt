Promotions and Coupons Policy
1. Overview

This document defines the rules governing promotional offers, discount coupons, and special deals available on the platform. These policies ensure fair usage, prevent abuse, and maintain consistency across customers, sellers, and campaigns.

Promotions may be platform-wide, seller-specific, or campaign-based and are subject to defined eligibility criteria.

2. Types of Promotions

The platform supports multiple types of promotional offers:

Coupon Codes: Applied manually at checkout
Auto-Applied Discounts: Automatically applied when conditions are met
Bank Offers: Discounts based on payment method
Seasonal Sales: Limited-time promotional campaigns
Bundle Offers: Discounts on combined purchases

Each type may have distinct terms and conditions.

3. Coupon Eligibility

Coupons are applicable only when all conditions are satisfied:

Minimum order value requirement
Applicable product categories
Valid user account status
Active validity period

Coupons may be restricted to:

New users only
Specific regions
Selected products or sellers
4. Coupon Application Rules

Customers must enter valid coupon codes during checkout. Key rules include:

Only one coupon may be applied per order unless explicitly stated
Coupons cannot be applied after order placement
Discounts are calculated before or after taxes depending on campaign rules
5. Coupon Expiry and Validity

All coupons are time-bound and include:

Start date and expiry date
Limited redemption count (first-come-first-served)

Expired coupons cannot be reinstated under normal circumstances.

6. Non-Stackable Promotions

In most cases, promotions cannot be combined:

Coupon + Coupon → Not allowed
Coupon + Bank Offer → Allowed in some cases
Platform Discount + Seller Discount → May be combined

Rules vary by campaign and are displayed at checkout.

7. Cancellation and Refund Impact

If an order using a coupon is cancelled:

Coupon may not be reissued
Refund amount excludes discount value

For partial cancellations:

Discount is recalculated proportionally
8. Abuse and Misuse Prevention

The platform actively monitors misuse of promotions, including:

Multiple account creation to reuse coupons
Exploiting pricing errors
Unauthorized coupon distribution

Consequences may include:

Coupon invalidation
Order cancellation
Account suspension
9. Seller-Specific Promotions

Marketplace sellers may offer their own discounts:

Seller coupons apply only to their products
Platform does not guarantee seller promotions
Seller terms may override general rules
10. Pricing Errors and Corrections

In case of incorrect pricing due to system errors:

Orders may be cancelled or corrected
Customers are notified before processing

The platform reserves the right to revoke unintended discounts.

11. Cashback and Wallet Offers

Some promotions provide cashback instead of instant discount:

Cashback credited after successful delivery
Subject to return/cancellation conditions
May have usage restrictions
12. Regional Restrictions

Certain promotions are limited by:

Geographic region
Shipping address
Local regulations

Users outside eligible regions cannot avail such offers.

13. Dispute Handling

If a promotion is not applied correctly:

Customers may contact support
Case reviewed based on campaign terms

If eligible, compensation may be provided in the form of:

Refund difference
Replacement coupon
14. Non-Transferable Nature

Coupons are:

Non-transferable
Non-exchangeable for cash
Valid only for the intended user
15. Policy Updates

Promotion rules may change based on campaigns, business strategies, or regulatory requirements.

16. Customer Support

Customers can reach support for issues related to promotions. Resolution timelines typically range between 24–72 hours.

Appendix C: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Q1: Why is my coupon not working?

A: It may not meet eligibility criteria or may have expired.

Q2: Can I use multiple coupons?

A: No, only one coupon can be applied per order.

Q3: Will I get refund on discounted amount?

A: Yes, refunds are based on final paid amount.

Q4: Can I reuse a coupon?

A: Only if it is still valid and unused.

Q5: What is cashback?

A: A partial refund credited after successful transaction.

Q6: When will cashback be credited?

A: Usually within a few days after order completion.

Q7: Can coupons be transferred?

A: No, coupons are non-transferable.

Q8: What happens if I cancel an order with coupon?

A: Coupon may be restored depending on terms.

Q9: Are coupons valid on all products?

A: No, some categories may be excluded.

Q10: Can I apply coupon after placing order?

A: No, coupons must be applied at checkout.

Q11: Why was my coupon removed?

A: It may violate terms or be flagged as misuse.

Q12: Are there special coupons for new users?

A: Yes, many platforms offer first-time user discounts.

Q13: Do coupons work on sale items?

A: Sometimes, depending on promotion rules.

Q14: Can I combine cashback and coupons?

A: In some cases, yes.

Q15: What if discount is not applied correctly?

A: Contact support for assistance.
