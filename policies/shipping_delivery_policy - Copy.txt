Shipping and Delivery Policy
1. Overview

This document defines the shipping, delivery, and logistics-related policies for orders placed on the platform. The objective is to ensure timely delivery, transparency in shipping costs, and clear procedures for handling delays or delivery failures.

Shipping timelines and charges may vary depending on product type, seller, and delivery location.

2. Shipping Methods

Orders are fulfilled through two primary methods:

First-party fulfillment (platform-managed warehouses)
Marketplace seller fulfillment

Each method may have different delivery timelines and service levels.

3. Delivery Timeframes

Estimated delivery dates are shown at the time of purchase. These estimates depend on:

Customer location
Seller location
Inventory availability
Courier partner efficiency

Typical delivery timelines:

Standard Shipping: 3–7 business days
Expedited Shipping: 1–3 business days
Remote Area Delivery: 5–10 business days

Delays may occur due to unforeseen circumstances such as weather conditions, strikes, or regulatory restrictions.

4. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated based on:

Order value
Delivery location
Product weight and dimensions

Free shipping may be available under certain conditions:

Orders above a specified value
Promotional campaigns
Subscription-based benefits

Shipping fees are displayed during checkout before payment confirmation.

5. Order Tracking

Once an order is shipped, customers receive tracking details via:

Email notifications
SMS alerts
Platform dashboard

Tracking information includes:

Shipment status
Estimated delivery date
Courier partner details
6. Delayed Deliveries

If an order is delayed beyond the estimated delivery date:

Customers may contact support for updates
Compensation may be provided in select cases (e.g., shipping refunds)

Delays do not automatically qualify for full refunds unless the item is no longer needed and return conditions are met.

7. Lost or Missing Packages

A package is considered lost if:

Tracking shows no movement for an extended period
Delivery is marked completed but not received by the customer

In such cases:

Investigation is initiated with the logistics partner
Resolution is provided within 3–7 business days

Possible outcomes:

Replacement shipment
Full refund
8. Failed Delivery Attempts

Delivery may fail due to:

Incorrect address
Customer unavailability
Access restrictions

After multiple failed attempts:

Order may be returned to origin
Refund or re-delivery options may be provided
9. International Shipping

For cross-border orders:

Additional charges may apply (customs, duties, taxes)
Delivery timelines may extend beyond standard estimates

Customers are responsible for complying with local import regulations.

10. Damaged During Transit

If a product is damaged during shipping:

Customer must report within 48 hours
Supporting evidence (photos/videos) may be required

Resolution options include:

Replacement
Refund
11. Address Modification

Customers may update delivery addresses before shipment. Once shipped, changes may not be possible.

12. Policy Updates

Shipping policies may be updated periodically. Customers are advised to review them before placing orders.

13. Customer Support

For shipping-related queries, customers can contact support. Resolution timelines typically range between 24–72 hours.

Appendix C: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Q1: How long does shipping take?

A: Typically 3–7 business days depending on location.

Q2: Can I track my order?

A: Yes, tracking details are provided after shipment.

Q3: What if my order is delayed?

A: You will be notified and may contact support.

Q4: Can I change my delivery address?

A: Only before the order is shipped.

Q5: What happens if I miss delivery?

A: Courier will attempt re-delivery or return to origin.

Q6: Are shipping charges refundable?

A: Only in cases of platform fault or failed delivery.

Q7: Do you deliver internationally?

A: Yes, but additional charges may apply.

Q8: What if my package is lost?

A: You will receive a refund or replacement.

Q9: Can I schedule delivery time?

A: This depends on courier availability.

Q10: What if my package is damaged?

A: Report immediately with proof for resolution.

Q11: Are there extra charges for remote areas?

A: In some cases, additional fees may apply.

Q12: Can someone else receive my package?

A: Yes, unless OTP or ID verification is required.

Q13: What if tracking is not updating?

A: It may take time; contact support if delay persists.

Q14: Is same-day delivery available?

A: Only in select locations.

Q15: What happens if delivery fails multiple times?

A: The order may be returned to origin.
