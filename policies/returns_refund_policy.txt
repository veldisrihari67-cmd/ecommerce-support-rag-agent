Returns and Refund Policy
1. Overview

This document outlines the return and refund procedures applicable to purchases made through an e-commerce platform. The policy is designed to ensure customer satisfaction while maintaining fairness for sellers and operational efficiency for logistics partners.

Customers are encouraged to review product-specific return eligibility before initiating a request, as policies may vary depending on item category, seller, and fulfillment method.

2. Return Eligibility Window

Most items are eligible for return within a period of 7 to 30 days from the date of delivery. The exact return window is displayed on the product page at the time of purchase.

Certain product categories such as electronics, fashion items, and home appliances may have varying return durations:

Electronics: Typically 7–10 days replacement window
Clothing & Accessories: Up to 30 days return period
Books and consumables: Limited or no return eligibility
3. Conditions for Return

To qualify for a return, the product must meet the following conditions:

Item must be unused and in original condition
All tags, labels, and packaging must be intact
Accessories, manuals, and warranty cards must be included
Product should not be damaged due to misuse

Returns may be rejected if the product does not meet these criteria.

4. Non-Returnable Items

The following categories are generally not eligible for return:

Perishable goods (food, flowers)
Personal care items (opened cosmetics, hygiene products)
Digital products and downloadable software
Customized or made-to-order items

In exceptional cases, defective or damaged items in these categories may be eligible for replacement.

5. Return Process

Customers can initiate a return by accessing their order history and selecting the relevant item. The process typically includes:

Selecting the reason for return
Choosing refund or replacement option
Scheduling pickup or selecting drop-off

Once initiated, a return request is processed within 24–48 hours.

6. Pickup and Shipping

Return pickup is arranged based on the customer’s location. In areas where pickup is unavailable, customers may be required to ship the product manually.

Shipping charges for returns:

Free returns for eligible items
Charges may apply for seller-fulfilled products
7. Refund Process

Refunds are initiated after the returned item is received and inspected. The timeline varies based on payment method:

UPI / Wallets: 2–4 business days
Debit/Credit Cards: 5–7 business days
Net Banking: 3–5 business days

Refunds may be issued as:

Original payment method
Store credit (in some cases)
8. Replacement Policy

For defective or damaged products, replacement may be offered instead of refund. Replacement is subject to stock availability.

If replacement is unavailable, a full refund will be processed.

9. Damaged or Defective Products

Customers must report damaged or defective items within 48 hours of delivery. Supporting images may be required for verification.

Failure to report within the specified timeframe may result in rejection of the request.

10. Late or Missing Refunds

If a refund has not been received:

Check bank account or wallet status
Contact payment provider
Reach out to customer support if delay exceeds expected timeline
11. Policy Updates

The platform reserves the right to modify return policies at any time. Customers are advised to review policies periodically.

12. Customer Support

For assistance, customers may contact support through chat, email, or helpline. Queries related to returns and refunds are typically resolved within 3–5 business days.

Appendix C: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Q1: Can I return a used product?

A: No, unless it is defective or damaged upon delivery.

Q2: What is the standard return window?

A: Usually 7–10 days depending on the product category.

Q3: How long does it take to receive a refund?

A: Typically 5–7 business days after approval.

Q4: Is original packaging required?

A: Yes, most returns require original packaging and tags.

Q5: Can I request an exchange instead of a refund?

A: Yes, exchanges are possible if stock is available.

Q6: Who pays for return shipping?

A: Platform pays for defective items; otherwise customer may bear the cost.

Q7: What happens if my return request is rejected?

A: The product will be sent back to you.

Q8: Can I cancel a return request?

A: Yes, before the pickup process is completed.

Q9: What if I miss the return window?

A: Returns may be denied unless an exception is approved.

Q10: Are international returns allowed?

A: Yes, but additional shipping charges may apply.

Q11: What should I do if my refund is delayed?

A: Contact customer support for assistance.

Q12: Can I return only part of my order?

A: Yes, partial returns are supported.

Q13: Are digital products refundable?

A: Generally no, unless there is a technical issue.

Q14: Can discounted products be returned?

A: Some discounted or clearance items may not be eligible.

Q15: How can I track my refund?

A: Refund status can be checked in your account dashboard.
