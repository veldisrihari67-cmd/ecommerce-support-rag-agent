Payment Policy
1. Overview

This document outlines the rules and procedures related to payments made on the platform. It covers accepted payment methods, transaction processing, failures, refunds, and fraud prevention mechanisms.

The objective is to ensure secure, transparent, and reliable payment handling for all users.

2. Accepted Payment Methods

The platform supports multiple payment options:

Credit and Debit Cards
UPI (Unified Payments Interface)
Net Banking
Digital Wallets
Cash on Delivery (COD) (subject to eligibility)

Availability of payment methods may vary based on:

Order value
Delivery location
User account status
3. Payment Authorization

All payments are subject to authorization by the respective financial institution.

Orders are confirmed only after:

Successful payment authorization
Verification of transaction details

Failed authorization results in order cancellation.

4. Payment Processing

Once a payment is initiated:

Funds may be temporarily held by the bank
Confirmation is sent upon successful processing
Invoice is generated for completed transactions

Processing times may vary depending on the payment method.

5. Failed Transactions

A transaction may fail due to:

Insufficient balance
Network issues
Bank authorization failure
Incorrect payment details

In such cases:

No order is confirmed
Held amount (if any) is released automatically

Customers are advised to retry or use an alternative payment method.

6. Duplicate Payments

If multiple payments are made for the same order:

Excess amount is identified automatically
Refund is initiated to the original payment source

Processing time depends on the payment provider.

7. Refund Processing

Refunds are initiated under the following scenarios:

Order cancellation
Return of products
Failed delivery
Payment errors

Refund timelines:

UPI/Wallet: 2–4 business days
Cards: 5–7 business days
Net Banking: 3–5 business days

Refunds are processed only after validation of eligibility.

8. Cash on Delivery (COD)

COD allows customers to pay at the time of delivery.

Conditions:

Available for select locations and products
May include additional service charges
Subject to order value limits

COD orders may be restricted for users with high return or cancellation rates.

9. Payment Security

The platform ensures secure transactions through:

Encryption technologies
Secure payment gateways
Fraud detection systems

Sensitive information such as card details is not stored without consent.

10. Fraud Detection and Prevention

The platform actively monitors transactions for suspicious activity, including:

Unusual transaction patterns
Multiple failed attempts
High-value orders from new accounts

Actions may include:

Transaction blocking
Account verification
Order cancellation
11. Chargebacks

If a customer disputes a charge through their bank:

Transaction is marked under investigation
Platform may request supporting documents

Unjustified chargebacks may result in:

Account restrictions
Legal action in severe cases
12. Payment Disputes

Customers may raise disputes for:

Incorrect charges
Failed transactions with deducted amount
Unauthorized payments

Support team reviews cases and provides resolution within 3–7 business days.

13. Partial Payments and Split Payments

Some orders may support:

Partial payments (e.g., wallet + card)
Split payments across methods

Refunds in such cases are processed proportionally.

14. Currency and Regional Restrictions

Transactions are processed in the applicable local currency.

International transactions may include:

Currency conversion charges
Additional bank fees
15. Taxes and Charges

Applicable taxes are included in the final price.

Additional charges may include:

Shipping fees
COD handling charges
Platform service fees

All charges are displayed before payment confirmation.

16. Payment Confirmation and Receipts

Customers receive:

Payment confirmation notification
Invoice or receipt

These can be accessed through the order history section.

17. Payment Failures After Deduction

In rare cases where payment is deducted but order is not confirmed:

Amount is automatically refunded
Timeline depends on bank processing

Customers may contact support if refund is delayed.

18. Account Restrictions

Accounts may be restricted for:

Repeated payment failures
Suspicious transactions
Chargeback abuse

Restrictions may limit access to certain payment methods.

19. Policy Updates

The platform reserves the right to update payment-related policies as required.

20. Customer Support

For payment-related issues, customers can contact support. Resolution timelines typically range between 24–72 hours.

Appendix C: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Q1: What payment methods are accepted?

A: Cards, UPI, net banking, wallets, and COD.

Q2: What if my payment fails?

A: You can retry or choose another method.

Q3: When will I get my refund?

A: Usually within 5–7 business days.

Q4: Can I change payment method after placing order?

A: No, but you can cancel and reorder.

Q5: What if I am charged twice?

A: Extra amount will be refunded.

Q6: Are payments secure?

A: Yes, processed through secure gateways.

Q7: Can I pay on delivery?

A: Yes, if COD is available.

Q8: What if refund is delayed?

A: Contact support for assistance.

Q9: Do I get refund in wallet or bank?

A: Refund is processed to original payment method.

Q10: Can international payments be refunded?

A: Yes, but processing may take longer.

Q11: What is a chargeback?

A: A dispute raised with your bank for transaction reversal.

Q12: Can I use multiple payment methods?

A: Generally not supported.

Q13: Are there extra charges for online payments?

A: Usually no, unless specified.

Q14: What if payment is pending?

A: Order will not be confirmed until payment is completed.

Q15: Can someone else pay for my order?

A: Yes, if payment details are valid.
