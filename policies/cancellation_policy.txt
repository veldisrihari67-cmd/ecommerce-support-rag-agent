Cancellation Policy
1. Overview

This document defines the rules and procedures governing order cancellations on the platform. It ensures transparency for customers while balancing operational constraints related to order processing, seller commitments, and logistics handling.

Order cancellation eligibility depends on the order status, fulfillment type, and product category.

2. Cancellation Window

Customers may cancel orders under the following conditions:

Before Shipment: Orders can typically be cancelled without restrictions.
After Shipment: Cancellation may not be possible; customers must initiate a return instead.
Out for Delivery: Cancellation is generally not allowed.

The exact cancellation window varies depending on:

Seller processing speed
Product category
Payment confirmation status
3. Cancellation Methods

Customers can cancel orders through:

Order history dashboard
Customer support assistance

Steps include:

Selecting the order
Choosing cancellation reason
Confirming cancellation request
4. Instant vs Approval-Based Cancellation

Orders may fall into two categories:

a) Instant Cancellation
Applicable for platform-fulfilled orders
Processed immediately without seller intervention
b) Seller Approval Required
Applies to marketplace orders
Seller reviews and approves/rejects the request
5. Refund for Cancelled Orders

Refunds for cancelled orders are processed based on payment method:

Prepaid Orders: Full refund initiated automatically
Cash on Delivery (COD): No charge applied if cancelled before delivery

Refund timelines:

Wallet/UPI: 2–4 days
Cards: 5–7 days
6. Partial Cancellation

Customers may cancel specific items within an order if:

Items are shipped separately
Seller supports item-level cancellation
7. Cancellation Restrictions

Cancellation may not be allowed for:

Personalized or customized products
Perishable items once processed
Digital goods or subscriptions
High-demand limited stock items
8. Failed Cancellation Attempts

If cancellation fails:

Customer must wait for delivery
Initiate return as per return policy
9. Auto-Cancellation by System

Orders may be automatically cancelled due to:

Payment failure
Stock unavailability
Seller inability to fulfill

Customers are notified and refunded where applicable.

10. Abuse Prevention

Frequent cancellations may trigger:

Account monitoring
Temporary restrictions

This ensures fair platform usage.

11. Cancellation After Delivery Attempt

If a customer refuses delivery:

Order is marked as cancelled
Refund processed after item returns to warehouse

Shipping charges may be deducted in certain cases.

12. Marketplace-Specific Conditions

For marketplace sellers:

Cancellation rules may vary
Seller policies override platform defaults in some cases
13. Dispute Scenarios

If cancellation is denied unfairly:

Customer may raise a dispute
Case reviewed by support team
14. Policy Updates

The platform reserves the right to update cancellation rules at any time.

15. Customer Support

Customers can contact support for cancellation-related issues. Resolution timelines range between 24–72 hours.

Appendix C: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Q1: Can I cancel my order anytime?

A: Only before the order is shipped.

Q2: Will I get a full refund on cancellation?

A: Yes, if canceled before shipment.

Q3: Can I cancel after shipping?

A: No, but you can return the item after delivery.

Q4: How long does refund take after cancellation?

A: Typically 5–7 business days.

Q5: Can I cancel part of my order?

A: Yes, partial cancellations are allowed.

Q6: What happens to COD orders if canceled?

A: No payment is processed; order is canceled.

Q7: Can I modify my order instead of canceling?

A: Modification may not be allowed; cancellation is recommended.

Q8: What if seller delays shipping?

A: You can cancel and get a full refund.

Q9: Will shipping charges be refunded?

A: Only if cancellation is due to platform or seller issue.

Q10: What if cancellation request fails?

A: Contact customer support immediately.

Q11: Can I cancel international orders?

A: Yes, but only before processing begins.

Q12: What if I placed duplicate orders?

A: You can cancel the extra order.

Q13: Is there a cancellation fee?

A: Usually no, unless specified.

Q14: What happens if refund is delayed?

A: Contact support for assistance.

Q15: Can seller cancel my order?

A: Yes, in case of stock or operational issues.
