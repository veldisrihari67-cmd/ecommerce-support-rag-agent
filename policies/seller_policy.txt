Seller Policy
1. Overview

This document defines the rules, responsibilities, and operational guidelines for third-party sellers operating on the platform. It establishes standards to ensure consistent product quality, reliable service, and fair treatment of customers.

The platform acts as a marketplace connecting customers with independent sellers while enforcing compliance with platform policies.

2. Seller Onboarding and Eligibility

To sell on the platform, sellers must:

Provide accurate business and identity information
Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
Agree to platform terms and policies

The platform reserves the right to approve or reject seller applications.

3. Product Listing Requirements

Sellers are responsible for maintaining accurate product listings, including:

Clear product descriptions
Correct pricing information
Authentic images
Category classification

Misleading or incorrect listings may result in penalties or removal.

4. Inventory Management

Sellers must ensure:

Accurate stock availability
Timely updates to inventory
Avoidance of overselling

Failure to maintain inventory may lead to order cancellations and performance penalties.

5. Order Fulfillment

Sellers must:

Process orders within defined timelines
Ship products using approved logistics partners
Provide tracking information

Delays in fulfillment may impact seller ratings and eligibility.

6. Shipping Responsibilities

For seller-fulfilled orders:

Sellers are responsible for packaging and shipping
Delivery timelines must match commitments
Proper labeling and documentation are required

Shipping delays may lead to disputes or penalties.

7. Returns and Refunds

Sellers must adhere to platform return and refund policies.

Responsibilities include:

Accepting eligible returns
Processing refunds within specified timelines
Coordinating with logistics for pickups

Sellers may define additional conditions, but they must not conflict with platform rules.

8. Replacement and Warranty

Sellers are responsible for:

Providing replacements for defective products
Honoring warranty commitments

Failure to comply may result in platform intervention.

9. Pricing and Promotions

Sellers may offer:

Discounts
Coupons
Promotional deals

However:

Pricing must remain transparent
Artificial inflation of prices before discounts is prohibited
10. Customer Communication

Sellers must:

Respond to customer queries promptly
Maintain professional communication
Avoid sharing sensitive or misleading information

Direct communication outside the platform is not allowed.

11. Performance Metrics

Seller performance is evaluated based on:

Order fulfillment rate
Cancellation rate
Return rate
Customer feedback and ratings

Poor performance may result in:

Reduced visibility
Suspension or termination
12. Policy Violations

Violations include:

Selling counterfeit or prohibited items
Misrepresentation of products
Failure to fulfill orders
Manipulating reviews

Penalties may include:

Listing removal
Account suspension
Financial penalties
13. Platform Intervention

The platform may intervene in disputes to:

Protect customer interests
Ensure policy compliance
Resolve conflicts between seller and buyer

In such cases, platform decisions are final.

14. Seller-Fulfilled vs Platform-Fulfilled Orders

Two fulfillment models exist:

a) Platform-Fulfilled
Inventory stored in platform warehouses
Platform handles shipping, returns, and customer support
b) Seller-Fulfilled
Seller manages storage, shipping, and initial support

Policies may differ based on fulfillment type.

15. Handling Disputes

Sellers must cooperate in dispute resolution by:

Providing required documentation
Responding within defined timelines

Failure to respond may result in automatic decisions in favor of the customer.

16. Refund Liability

In marketplace transactions:

Sellers may bear refund costs
Platform may recover funds from seller accounts

This depends on fault determination.

17. Restricted and Prohibited Products

Sellers are prohibited from listing:

Illegal items
Hazardous materials
Counterfeit goods

Violations may lead to immediate account termination.

18. Account Suspension and Termination

Seller accounts may be suspended or terminated for:

Policy violations
Fraudulent activity
Repeated poor performance

Reinstatement is subject to review.

19. Data Privacy and Security

Sellers must:

Protect customer data
Use data only for order fulfillment
Avoid unauthorized sharing

Non-compliance may lead to legal consequences.

20. Fees and Charges

Sellers may be subject to:

Listing fees
Commission charges
Shipping fees

Details are provided during onboarding.

21. Policy Updates

Seller policies may be updated periodically. Sellers are responsible for staying informed.

22. Customer Support Coordination

Sellers must coordinate with platform support teams to ensure timely resolution of customer issues.

23. Compliance and Legal Obligations

Sellers must comply with all applicable laws, including:

Consumer protection laws
Tax regulations
Product safety standards
24. Final Authority

The platform reserves the right to make final decisions in all seller-related matters.

25. Contact and Support

Sellers may contact platform support for assistance related to policies, disputes, and operational issues.
